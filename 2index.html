What are the taxes like in UAE, Why did they implement taxes and has the Economy Beneifted from taxes?

Like I briefly talked about in the other page taxes are very minimal in UAE and they were only implemented very recently. The tax is called VAT (value added tax) and it is an added 5 percent on whatever you buy. UAE had to implement taxes because they had taken a loan from the world bank and they decided that UAE wouldnt be able to pay it all back when they watned them to so they implented VAT. The one major downside has been the huge suffering for the economy of UAE, even though the market was bad before VAT after VAT it has become worse.
